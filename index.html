Overview

Crapette, also known as Russian Bank, is a competitive patience card game for two players. The objective is to be the first to move all your reserve cards to the foundation piles.

Rules

The game is played with two standard 52-card decks. Each player deals 13 cards into a reserve pile and places the remaining cards in a stock pile. Players take turns to move cards to the foundation piles or tableau.

Setup

  1. Shuffle your deck and deal 13 cards face down as your reserve pile, with the top card face up.
  2. Place the remaining 39 cards face down as your stock pile.
  3. Deal four cards face up next to your reserve pile as your tableau.

How to Play

On your turn, you can:

  • Move the top card of your reserve pile to a foundation or tableau pile.
  • Move cards between tableau piles.
  • Draw from your stock pile if no other moves are possible.

The game continues until one player moves all their reserve cards to the foundations.
